Frequently Asked Questions

How does the robot access our gated community?

We coordinate access with your board — same as any vendor. The robot operates on community streets only, following the same routes a resident or delivery driver would take.

What about resident privacy?

We photograph property exteriors from the street — the same view any pedestrian has. No backyard access, no windows, no faces. Images are used for compliance documentation only and stored securely.

Do we still make enforcement decisions?

Absolutely. BeeSafe surfaces potential violations for your review. You decide what gets a notice and what gets dismissed. Human judgment, automated documentation.

What violations can it detect?

Common visible violations: trash cans left out, open garage doors, vehicles on lawns, RVs/boats in driveways, landscaping anomalies, and more. We're not replacing your judgment — we're giving you consistent coverage.

What does it cost?

Pricing depends on community size and patrol frequency. Most communities pay $400–900/month. Schedule a demo and we'll provide a custom quote based on your specific needs.
